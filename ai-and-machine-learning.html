What We Do

Image and Video Recognition

We specialize in the development of image and video recognition systems of various complexity. We solve object detection and classification tasks for both photo and video formats.

OCR

We build applications powered by optical character recognition for a quick and effective visual data analysis. Our portfolio includes projects of various complexity - from document digitizing and conversion to special character location and categorisation.

NLP

We are well-versed in the development of natural language analysis and generation systems. From information extraction to sentiment analysis and text generation, we leverage the latest technologies to help you get the most out of your data set.

AR

Augmented reality offers a wide range of experiences that revolutionise business. Our AR team are experts in analysing business issues and developing innovative solutions that elevates your business to the next level.

Transforming Business With Artificial Intelligence

Document Recognition

We take full advantage of the latest OCR technologies to create applications that perform complex tasks in a matter of minutes. We develop systems that can analyse scanned documents of any complexity, including technical drawings and blueprints, detect and count symbols, as well as extract the data for further analysis.

Live Video Stream Analysis

Systems for analysis of live video streams in order to detect and classify objects present in a video. Our applications are used in a large number of fields - from monitoring at-home 3D printing to detecting a safe waterway for commercial ships and monitoring of continuous industrial processes to detect malfunctions for safety and quality control.

Marketing Content Generation

Generation of marketing content, like social media captions, promotion leaflets and scripts for promotional video content, powered by natural language processing technologies

AI for Health and Wellness

Machine learning can take personal wellness and health applications to the next level. We have built a number of systems powered by computer vision and machine learning for the wellness industry, like eye training app with eye tracking.
